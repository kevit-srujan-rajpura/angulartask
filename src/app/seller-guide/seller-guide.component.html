Seller Guide

1) Create your MyStore seller acoount from homepage of MyStore and be ready with your GST number.
2) Fill all the required field in Product form and list your product in MyStore.
3) You can also delete your product as well update your product for more selles from Manage products section.
4) Once you become a verified MyStore seller, payments are deposited safely to your bank account every 7 days, even for Pay on Delivery orders.
